About the brand


Our vision

In a world where women feel that they stand alone against men, we believe that women should stand together and give a hand where needed no matter their background, only then we will achieve total inclusion and stand together for feminism.


Our mission

For a long time women have been standing alone against men, we want all women to feel that our brand offers them the opportunity to feel included no matter what their background is. Standing together for feminism!
